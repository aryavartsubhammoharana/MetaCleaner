Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2, 2026

At MetaCleaner, privacy is not a feature—it is the foundation of our existence. This Privacy Policy describes how we process files and metadata, and why our system guarantees your total confidentiality.

1. Zero Storage Policy

We do not store your files. When you upload a file, it is saved in a secured temporary directory on our servers with a randomly generated unique identifier. It is only held long enough to read its metadata and clean it.

2. Automatic Immediate Deletion

As soon as you click the Download button, the cleaned file and the original file are permanently and immediately deleted from our disk drives. This happens at the file system level in real-time. If you close your browser or navigate away without downloading, our server-side automated cron task wipes all uploaded and cleaned files older than 15 minutes.

3. No File Logging or Inspection

We do not inspect, catalog, index, or parse the actual contents of your uploads. We only read standard metadata container elements (such as EXIF, ID3, or docProps) to present them to you. We do not keep logs of file names, contents, metadata, or geographic coordinates extracted during the process.

4. No User Tracking or Accounts

MetaCleaner does not require, support, or allow user registration. There are no profiles, user accounts, or login forms. We do not use persistent tracking cookies or analytics tools that compromise your digital fingerprint.

5. HTTPS and Security

All communication between your browser and our server is secured using SSL/TLS encryption (HTTPS). This prevents third parties from eavesdropping on your file transfers.

Reminder: Your files are processed temporarily and automatically deleted after download.
